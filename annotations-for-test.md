## cURL commands  
- **GET**  - curl.exe http://localhost:8080/ -v  
- **POST** - curl.exe http://localhost:8080/ -v -d "POST data"
- **PUT**  - curl.exe -X PUT http://localhost:8080/ -d "PUT data" -v
